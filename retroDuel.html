Retro Duel

Retro Duel is a local two player fencing game in which you battle your way to victory atop an ever-shrinking stage. Use a mix of parries, thrusts, dodges, and finesse to defeat your friends and claim the title of champion.

Choose from a variety of swords and game modes which keep each round fresh and exciting. Play to your sword’s strength while exploiting the weakness of your foe’s sword to gain an edge.

To Play

  1. Dowload

  2. Unzip downloaded files

  3. Run Retro Duel from the extracted folder
